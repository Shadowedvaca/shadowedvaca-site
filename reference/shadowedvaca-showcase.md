# shadowedvaca.com — Project Showcase

All items status: **Idea**

## Games

- **The Fraying** — A horror game that listens to your conversations and uses your vulnerability against you.
- **Threads** — Explore a patient's mindscape across therapy sessions where the world changes as trust deepens.
- **The Tome** — Your map is alive, it has its own agenda, and it's learning to lie to you.
- **Convergence** — A co-op game where you hold your partner's map in a world that's falling apart around you.
- **Dueling Decks** — A two-player card game where your deck is your life force — every upgrade brings you closer to death.

## Systems

- **NPC Chatbots** — A browser-based tool for writers to build and test LLM-powered characters through interactive mind maps.
- **Book Club** — A voting and scheduling app for book clubs with multi-channel notifications.
- **Game Group OS** — A game night intelligence platform that knows what your group knows and makes organizing effortless.
- **Wishlist** — A personal wishlist tool with instant capture from anywhere — replacing Amazon, owned by you.
- **Journal** — A minimal, encrypted journaling app built for quick entries on any device.
- **Complexify** — A text tool that replaces words with fancier synonyms and learns your vocabulary preferences over time.

## Creative Writing

- **Riven** — A boy whose mind exists in two realities — one dying, one thriving — and only he can see both.
- **Tick Tock** — A family discovers the old man they're caring for is a creature that feeds on fear by warping reality.
- **Side Quest** — A hero's journey told by people who never noticed the hero — each narrator is too busy being the hero of their own story.
- **Psychic Neighbors** — A grandmother's coup, a psychic cold war, and a property line that keeps moving.
- **Poetry** — A collection of poems from a transformative period.

## 3D Printing

- **Next Player** — A physical WiFi-connected button for tracking turns at board game night.
