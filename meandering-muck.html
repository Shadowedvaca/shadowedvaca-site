What Is Meandering Muck?

Meandering Muck is a tilt-controlled maze game. You navigate a slime through procedurally generated mazes using your phone's motion controls. Think Cut the Rope or Threes: a simple puzzle premise, done well.

The maze generation algorithm has been refined over ten years. Every maze is unique. Every maze is solvable. The challenge is the maze itself‚Äîthe reward is solving it.

There are no enemies. No combat. No death. The slimes are pacifists. This is a puzzle game in the tradition of Monument Valley, Threes, and Cut the Rope‚Äîgames you pick up, play, put down, and come back to.
