Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 26, 2026

We don't collect your data. Period. Meandering Muck is designed to respect your privacy completely.

What We Don't Collect

Meandering Muck does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, etc.)
  • Device identifiers or fingerprints
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Any data for sale to third parties

Local Data Storage

Your game progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your phone and is not accessible to us or any third party.

If you uninstall the game, this local data is deleted.

Platform Services

Meandering Muck integrates with platform services for optional features:

  • Apple Game Center (iOS): If you choose to sign in, your Game Center display name and scores are shared with Apple's leaderboard system. This is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Games (Android): If you choose to sign in, your Play Games display name and scores are shared with Google's leaderboard system. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

These integrations are entirely optional. The game functions fully without signing into either service.

No Ads, No Tracking

Meandering Muck contains no advertisements. We do not use any advertising SDKs, tracking pixels, or analytics services. There is no code in our app that reports your behavior to us or anyone else.

Children's Privacy

Because we don't collect any data, there is no special concern regarding children's privacy. The game is safe for players of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update this page with the new date. However, our commitment to not collecting your data is fundamental to who we are. We don't anticipate this changing.
