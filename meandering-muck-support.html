Common Questions

The tilt controls feel off. How do I fix them?

Go to Settings → Controls → Calibrate. Hold your phone in your natural playing position and tap "Set." You can also adjust the sensitivity sliders to match your preference.

I lost my progress after reinstalling. Can you restore it?

Unfortunately, game progress is stored locally on your device and we don't have access to it. If you signed into Game Center (iOS) or Google Play Games (Android), your leaderboard scores and achievements are preserved, but level progress is local only.

Does the game work offline?

Yes! Meandering Muck works 100% offline. Leaderboards and achievements will sync the next time you're connected, but gameplay works anywhere.

How do I turn off the timer?

Go to Settings → Gameplay and change "Play Your Way" from Competitive to Cozy. This removes timers, trophies, and leaderboards.
